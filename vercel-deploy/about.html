The Artist

Anastasiia
Lykhosherstova

Born in Kyiv and trained at the conservatoire, Anastasiia has performed across concert halls and private estates from Prague to Paris. Her repertoire spans Baroque through to contemporary, with a rare ability to read the emotional need of each occasion.

She brings not only technical precision but genuine warmth to every performance — the ability to make a room fall silent, or move an audience to tears with a single phrase. Whether accompanying a first kiss at the altar or filling a grand ballroom with sound, each performance is shaped with the same quiet care.

Her versatility is one of her most valued qualities. Classical, cinematic, contemporary pop — Anastasiia curates a repertoire unique to every event, always in conversation with the couple or host.
