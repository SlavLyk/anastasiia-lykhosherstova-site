The Artist

Anastasiia
Lykhosherstova

Born in Ukraine, Anastasiia is an accomplished violinist and composer based in Calgary, Alberta, currently working as a composer for a video game company while maintaining an active performance career. With over two decades of musical training and professional experience spanning classical performance and contemporary composition, she brings a unique blend of traditional artistry and modern creative innovation to her work.

Her professional career spans multiple prestigious ensembles across Ukraine and Canada. In Ukraine, she served as Concertmaster of the University of Arts Symphony Orchestra and held principal violin positions with the Youth Academic Symphony Orchestra "Slobozhansky" and Chamber Orchestra "Nova Sinfonietta." From 2019 to 2021, she served as an Artist of Trio with the Kharkiv Philharmonic.

After relocating to Canada, she continued her distinguished career as a member of the Symphony of the Kootenays and served as Concertmaster of the Rocky Mountain Symphony Orchestra. She has been featured at the Mountain View Festival in 2024 and 2025. Currently, Anastasiia composes immersive musical experiences for interactive media, a natural evolution combining her deep classical training with contemporary digital composition techniques.
